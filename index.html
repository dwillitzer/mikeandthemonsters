The Band


EVERYTHING your party/rock soul can handle in one show!!!
Mike and the Monsters

*BE SURE TO CLICK ON OUR VIDEO PAGE FOR DEMO!*

“A female fronted Five piece rock powerhouse”

Mike and the Monsters cover a wide range and variety of music, performed with energy, professionalism, style and true rock and roll conviction! Modern sounds, classic rock and maximum danceable party rock make up the Monsters musical repertoire. We are an extremely INTERACTIVE band!!! Audience participation is a MUST at a Mike and the Monster's show!!! So, dont be shy! Cause we're gonna have you singing, dancing, playing a little air guitar and having a few toasts with our audience, whether  it be a birthday, anniversay, or just because it's 5 O'clock...somewhere!!! A live experience with "Mike and the Monsters" is like being part of the band!!

All Monster members are experienced Capital District and regional music veterans, coming together with one goal " to deliver the goods“ with a great live show and to keep people coming back for more! Deep musicianship, multi-vocals along with the great selling point and secret weapon in lead vocalist Michaele Ann Flynn, build a solid foundation for a Monster house of good times and great music!!!

Michaele Ann Flynn "Mike"
Lead Vocalist

Hi ya! I'm Michaele Ann (aka - Mike) and I have been a long time rocker in the upstate capital area. As you can tell by all the photos.. I'm infamous for changing my "Look!" (Got to keep it FRESH!)over the years I have fronted several original and cover bands performing from Albany, N.Y. and it's surrounding cities including  Massachusettes, Boston, Syracuse, and New York City. The band I have been  noted for is "Tokyo Rose" also known as "Shovelhead." This original band of 9 years had an "Evanescence/Pantera" feel. With Shovelhead in the past, I took a short break. Needing to get back to performing, I opted to build a band complete with some of the most established and well rounded players in the area. Creating one of the best Pop/Rock cover bands in the area. As a front person, my goal is to be as interactive with our audience as possible!! I may borrow your hat, have a seat on your lap, share some wings, have a shot, dance on the bar, or have a chorus join me on stage!! WHATEVER  strikes my mood at the moment!! Life should be a party! At least for the few hours you spend with US!!  :-)"Mike (ME)... and the Monsters" are rockin everyone with the newest, hottest covers, and the best classic's! See you at the next show!! Peace & Love!!! Mike!!! (BTW - YES..Mike is my real name!)
